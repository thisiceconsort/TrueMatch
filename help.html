Help & Support

Welcome to the TrueMatch Creations Help Center. Below you'll find answers to common questions and ways to contact us for further assistance.

Frequently Asked Questions

quiz How do I create a profile or account?

To create an account, click on the "Home" button in the bottom navigation bar and click "Join Community" (if applicable). Follow the prompts to set up your account.

shopping_cart How do I purchase items from the shop?

Browse our "Shop" section. Once you find an item you like, click on it to view details and then follow the steps to add it to your cart and proceed with checkout.

article Where can I find articles or news?

Our "Resources" section (accessible via the top right icon) contains articles, blogs, and news updates. Select "Article, Blog, News" from the menu.

eco Tell me more about the Agriculture Initiative.

Our Agriculture Initiative is currently under development. It's designed to help LGBTQ+ land owners utilize their land for livestock rearing or high-value crops. You can find more details on the "Agriculture" page or by emailing us directly for inquiries.

policy Where can I find legal information or terms of service?

All legal information, including our privacy policy and terms of service, can be found by clicking on the "Legal" icon in the bottom navigation bar.
