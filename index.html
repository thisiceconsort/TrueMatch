Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mìlàájé all about? keyboard_arrow_down

Mìlàájé is a dedicated online platform designed to foster connection among verified members of the LGBTQ+ community across Africa. Our primary objective is to create a safe, supportive, and authentic environment for individuals within this community. The Service operates on a stringent verification system to ensure the integrity and safety of its members.

Why do I need to pay to join? keyboard_arrow_down

You're actually not paying to join — joining the Mìlàájé community is and will always be free. What you're doing is making a one-time, mandatory support contribution to help grow the community. Your support goes into empowering LGBTQ+ members across Africa, running advocacy programs in countries with stricter laws, creating skill-based learning opportunities, and maintaining a secure and welcoming platform for everyone.

What are the privileges of joining this community? keyboard_arrow_down

The privileges are countless! You’ll be able to connect with like-minded LGBTQ+ folks within your own country, state/province, or even local government area. We have a growing online shop to empower digital creators — especially writers and filmmakers — to earn from their work. You’ll get access to courses in areas like building your own website without code, email marketing, and social media strategy. Our future plans include an agricultural initiative to support LGBTQ+ landowners in maximizing their land, and the development of our fashion/modelling department for those interested in these fields. Most excitingly, we’re building a secure mobile app (iOS & Android) to make group interactions and one-on-one chats with like-minded people even more accessible.

How do I use this website? keyboard_arrow_down

By just visiting this page, you're already on track — we’ve made the entire website easy to navigate with clear icons and smooth browsing.

To verify a card or a user, simply enter the 4-digit Mìlàájé code found on their card into the verification field. This helps ensure they’re truly part of the secure community. Remember: this only works if both you and the other person are part of Mìlàájé. If they don’t have a card, share the link with them and ask them to create one. If they refuse, stop chatting. It’s for your own safety.

On our shop, we offer both digital and physical products at very affordable prices. Deliveries are discreet and fast. Please note: there are no refunds for digital products.
