Shop Policies ↑

Our shop operates on a unique payment and delivery system designed to give you control and ensure a seamless experience.

10. Shop

The Mìlàájé Shop offers a curated selection of products, combining community-created content with affiliated products.

10.1. Community Creative Content (Books & Movies)

  • Verified members are granted the exclusive privilege to share and monetize their creative works, including books and movies, within the Shop. This serves as a potential source of recurring income for creators and establishes a centralized global marketplace for unique creative content.
  • Deliveries for digital creative works are typically immediate.
  • For every sale of your creative content, **Mìlàájé will retain a 27% commission** to cover platform maintenance, processing fees, and support. The remaining 73% will be paid directly to you.
  • Each book or movie you submit for sale on the platform must be registered. The amount you pay for registration is the exact price you will set for your content. For a higher-priced book or movie, you will need to pay that same amount as a registration fee. We believe that if you value your content enough to pay for its registration, other like-minded individuals in our community will also be willing to pay that price to access it. However, always remember to be price competitive, as buyers usually opt for more affordably priced products.
  • All sales of digital creative works are final, and no refunds are processed for these items.

10.2. Affiliated Products (AliExpress)

  • Mìlàájé features a selection of affiliated products sourced primarily from AliExpress. These products are not owned or directly sold by Mìlàájé. Given the diverse needs of the LGBTQ+ community across Africa, AliExpress has been chosen for its wide product range, global delivery capabilities, security, and discreet shipping.
  • When you select an affiliated product, you will be redirected to the AliExpress website or mobile application (if installed) to complete your purchase.
  • For every affiliated product purchased through our platform, the Mìlàájé community earns a commission. This allows members to support the community while acquiring useful and often cost-effective products.
  • Delivery times for affiliated products can range from 7 days to 2 weeks, depending on the product and location.
  • Refund policies for affiliated products are subject to AliExpress's terms and conditions.

Please note that our shop only showcases selected products from AliExpress. We do not sell these products directly. Before making any purchase, we strongly encourage you to make proper enquiries about the product to ensure it meets your needs.

While we make every effort to feature only trusted sellers and quality products, we are not responsible for any issues related to your order.

10.3. How to Use the Shop

  • You can search for products using the search bar or browse available items.
  • Click on a product to view details and add it to your cart.
  • The cart icon, located at the top right of the screen, displays your selected items and their total cost. Click on the cart to review your selections.
  • Clicking anywhere else on the screen will close the cart, allowing you to proceed to checkout.
