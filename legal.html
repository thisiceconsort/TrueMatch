    3. On the new payment page, you will be prompted to **enter the product code**. This code is crucial for us to identify and deliver the correct item to you.
    4. Proceed with your payment. Once confirmed, we will ensure the delivery of your selected book or movie.
  • Creating and Selling Your Own Content (Books/Movies): We empower creators by giving you full control over your content's value:
    • When you decide to add and **register your own book or movie** on our platform, you will be asked to **input your desired price**.

Donations are entirely at your discretion, and you can **input any amount you wish to contribute**.
